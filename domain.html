EDUCATION SECTOR

SDG Target 4.1

Focus: Ensure that all youth and a substantial proportion of adults, both men and women, achieve literacy and numeracy.

Indicator 4.1.1: Proportion of youth and adults who have achieved at least a minimum level of proficiency in literacy and numeracy, by gender.

SDG Target 4.4

Focus: Increase the number of youth and adults who have relevant skills for employment, decent jobs, and entrepreneurship.

Indicator 4.4.1: Proportion of youth and adults with ICT skills, by gender.

GARBAGE HANDLING

SDG Target 11.6

Focus: Reduce the adverse per capita environmental impact of cities, including by paying special attention to air quality and municipal and other waste management.

Indicator 11.6.1: Proportion of urban solid waste regularly collected and with adequate final discharge out of total urban solid waste, by cities.

SDG Target 12.5

Focus: Substantially reduce waste generation through prevention, reduction, recycling, and reuse.

Indicator 12.5.1: National recycling rate, tons of material recycled.

SDG Target 13.2

Focus: Integrate climate change measures into national policies, strategies, and planning.

Indicator 13.2.1: Number of countries that have communicated the establishment or operationalization of an integrated policy/strategy/plan which increases their ability to adapt to the adverse impacts of climate change, and foster climate resilience.
