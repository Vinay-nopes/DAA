CASE STUDY CITY DESIGN

NAME OF THE CITY

INDUS CITY

SUMMARY:


Our designed city, Indus, reimagines Bangalore. *Click here to know more* with innovative concepts inspired by the Design and Analysis of Algorithms course. Spanning an area of 1,500 sq. km, the city is meticulously divided into functional zones:

  • Residential Zone: 45%
  • Commercial and Industrial Zones: 30%
  • Public Spaces and Institutional Areas: 10%
  • Green Spaces and Residential Areas: 10%
  • Transport Infrastructure: 5%
Designed to be self-sustainable, Indus accommodates a population of approximately 6,000,000 with an average density of 6,000 per sq km and the capacity to host an additional 100,000 migrants and tourists. Its revenue models and sustainability initiatives ensure long-term viability and resilience, making it a modern Indus Valley Civilization that balances growth with ecological harmony.
